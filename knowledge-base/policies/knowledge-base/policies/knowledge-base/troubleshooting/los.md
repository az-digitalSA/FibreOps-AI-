# Loss of Signal (LOS)

## Description

A Loss of Signal (LOS) fault indicates that the Optical Network Terminal (ONT) is not receiving a valid optical signal from the Fibre Network Operator (FNO).

## Customer Symptoms

- No internet connectivity
- LOS LED is solid red or flashing red
- Router may indicate no WAN connection

## Possible Causes

- Damaged drop fibre
- Broken fibre cable
- Loose fibre connector
- Dirty fibre connector
- Faulty splice
- Distribution or feeder fibre fault
- OLT outage
- Damaged ONT
- Planned maintenance

## Initial Troubleshooting

- Confirm LOS LED status
- Reboot the ONT
- Perform router bypass
- Check fibre patch lead
- Confirm whether neighbouring customers are affected
- Check for planned maintenance

## Information Required Before Escalation

- ONT serial number
- ONT LED status
- Optical power readings (if available)
- Photos of the ONT LEDs
- Technician findings
- Area affected
- Existing FNO reference (if available)

## Escalation Criteria

Escalate when:

- LOS remains after troubleshooting
- Technician confirms no customer-side fault
- Physical fibre damage is suspected
- OLT or distribution fault is suspected

## Expected Outcome

The FNO investigates the optical path and restores signal.
