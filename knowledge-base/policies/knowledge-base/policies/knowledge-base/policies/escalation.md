# Fibre Escalation Policy

## Purpose

This document defines when a Fibre Operations ticket should be escalated to a Fibre Network Operator (FNO).

---

## Required Information Before Escalation

The following information should be available before escalating:

- Client name
- Account number
- Contact number
- FNO
- Circuit ID (if available)
- ONT serial number (if applicable)
- Description of the fault
- Time the issue started
- Troubleshooting already performed

---

## General Escalation Criteria

Escalate when:

- Standard troubleshooting has been completed.
- The issue appears to be outside Afrihost's network.
- The customer equipment has been ruled out.
- An FNO investigation is required.

---

## Do Not Escalate When

Do not escalate if:

- The router is powered off.
- The customer has not completed basic troubleshooting.
- The issue is caused by customer equipment.
- Required information is missing.

---

## Information Required in Technician Notes

Technician notes should include:

- Fault summary
- Troubleshooting completed
- Current status
- Reason for escalation
- Information still outstanding
